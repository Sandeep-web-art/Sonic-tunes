Indian Pop

Indian pop music, often referred to as Indi-pop, includes a variety of genres and has gained significant popularity since the 1990s, featuring artists like Alisha Chinai and Daler Mehndi.

International Pop

International pop music encompasses a wide range of genres from around the world, including mainstream artists like Taylor Swift and Ed Sheeran, shaping the global music landscape.

Punjabi Pop

Punjabi pop is a vibrant genre characterized by upbeat rhythms and catchy lyrics, popularized by artists like Gippy Grewal and Sidhu Moosewala.
